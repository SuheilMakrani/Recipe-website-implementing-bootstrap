Glossary

Term

Definition
Brush An act of sweeping, applying, or arranging with a brush or with one's hand.
Crumble Cause (something) to break apart into small fragments.
Coat Provide with a layer or covering of something.
Cook Prepare (food, a dish, or a meal) by mixing, combining, and heating the ingredients.
Deep-fry Fry (food) in an amount of fat or oil sufficient to cover it completely.
Disperse Distribute or spread over a wide area.
Frying Cook (food) in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan.
Grill Cook (food) using a grill.
Lengthwise Cutting along the length of a substance
Mash Reduce (a food or other substance) to a pulpy mass by crushing it.
Melt Make or become liquefied by heating.
Microwave Cook (food) in a microwave oven.
Refrigerate Subject (food or drink) to cold in order to chill or preserve it, typically by placing it in a refrigerator.
Rinse Remove (soap, detergent, dirt, or impurities) by washing with clean water.
Season Add salt, herbs, pepper, or other spices to (food).
Skillet A small metal cooking pot with a long handle, typically having legs.
Sprinkle Cover (an object or surface) with small drops or particles of a substance.
Spoon Put (food) into or on something with a spoon.
Stir Move a spoon or other implement round and round in (a liquid or other substance) in order to mix it thoroughly.
Whisk Beat or stir (a substance, especially cream or eggs) with a light, rapid movement.
