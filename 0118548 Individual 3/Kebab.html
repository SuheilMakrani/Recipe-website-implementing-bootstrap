Kebab

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound fingerling potatoes, halved lengthwise

  • 1/2 pound beef tenderloin, cut into 1 1/2 inch cube

  • 2 tablespoon extra virgin oil

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

  • 1 bunch asparagus, cut into 1 to 2 inch pieces

  • 1/4 cup ketchup

  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard

  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

  • 2 teaspoons steak sauce

  • Pinch of red pepper flakes







Directions

  1. Preheat a grill to medium high and brush the grates with vegetable oil.

  2. Put the potatoes in a microwave-safe bowl, cover and microwave until just tender, about 5 minutes.

  3. Toss the beef with the olive oil in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.

  4. Thread the beef, potatoes and asparagus onto eight 10-inch skewers.

  5. Combine the ketchup, mustard, vinegar, 2 tablespoons water, the Worcestershire sauce, steak sauce and red pepper flakes in a small bowl.

  6. Grill the kebabs, turning occasionally, until marked, about 6 minutes for medium rare. Serve with the sauce.




Total: 30 min
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 15 min
Yield: 4 servings
Level: Easy
